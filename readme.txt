English


This resource pack changes some durability levels of diamond sword into warplanes.

You can summon the warplanes with any attitude by using below comamnd:

/summon armor_stand ~ ~1 ~ {HandItems:[{id:diamond_sword,Count:1b,tag:{Unbreakable:1,Damage:'DURABILITY'}},{}],NoGravity:1b,Invisible:1,Tags:[aircraft],Pose:{RightArm:['PITCH-90.0'f,0.0f,'ROLL'f]},Rotation:['YAW'f,0.0f]}

DURABILITY 	:durability level of diamond sword
PITCH 		:pitch angle of the aircraft
YAW 		:yaw angle of the aircraft
ROLL 		:roll angle of the aircraft

exemple:
/summon armor_stand ~ ~1 ~ {HandItems:[{id:diamond_sword,Count:1b,tag:{Damage:1,Unbreakable:1}},{}],NoGravity:1b,Invisible:1,Tags:[aircraft],Pose:{RightArm:[-102f,0f,0f]},Rotation:[0.0f,0.0f]}


Note: Some models divide into multiple parts due to model's size limit.

日本語


このリソースパックはダイヤの剣の見た目を航空機に変えるリソースパックです。

航空機は以下のコマンドを使って召喚できます。

/summon armor_stand ~ ~1 ~ {HandItems:[{id:diamond_sword,Count:1b,tag:{Unbreakable:1,Damage:'DURABILITY'}},{}],NoGravity:1b,Invisible:1,Tags:[aircraft],Pose:{RightArm:['PITCH-90.0'f,0.0f,'ROLL'f],Rotation:['YAW'f,0.0f]}}

DURABILITY	:ダイヤの剣の耐久
PITCH 		:航空機のピッチ角
YAW			:航空機のヨー角
ROLL 		:航空機のロール角

コマンド例：
/summon armor_stand ~ ~1 ~ {HandItems:[{id:diamond_sword,Count:1b,tag:{Damage:1,Unbreakable:1}},{}],NoGravity:1b,Invisible:1,Tags:[aircraft],Pose:{RightArm:[-12f,0f,0f]},Rotation:[0.0f,0.0f]}

注意：いくつかのモデルはサイズ制限のため複数のパーツに分割しています
